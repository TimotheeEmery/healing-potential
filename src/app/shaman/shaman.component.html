Welcome to the healing potential app for shamans
What is it?

Have you ever compare two items and wondered for a moment which one would benefit the most your healing abilities? If your answer is yes, you've come to the right place! The healing potential app is made to compute how much HP you can heal through a whole fight by informing the key factors. By varying those factors you can easily see the effect on the healing potential and assess which ones matter the most.

How to use it?

Simply inform the variables and modifiers below before clicking on the "Compute score" button, The result will be shown below it. The healing potential represents the number of hp you can heal during the whole fight. The detailed results shows which ranks will be used, and the value of each stats, which is how much healing potential it would add by having 1 more of that stat.

How is it done?
It will be explained in another section of this site soon. For now here's the main assumptions:
  • You finish the fight with as few mana as possible
  • You are casting non-stop (therefore no regen related to spirit because of the five second rule)
  • You have more than 300 heal bonus (because it expects downranked spells to be the most mana efficient)
  • You have all the talents that are useful for healing
Don't hesitate to hover the all over the place for more infos.
